What is Bookswap?

University of Notre Dame IDEA Center backed startup to help you buy and sell used textbooks

Vision:

Help students sell their textbooks to other Notre Dame students. Save money with Bookswap!

Goals:

  • Reduce the environmental impact of printing new books over and over again
  • Help students sell their unwanted textbooks for a significantly better value than bookstore buyback
  • Help students buy used textbooks for less money than the bookstore
